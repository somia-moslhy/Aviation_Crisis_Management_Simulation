# Aviation Crisis Management Simulation

[![Streamlit App](https://static.streamlit.io/badges/streamlit_badge_black_white.svg)](https://aviationcrisismanagementsimulation-g8tdpjvenk879p6dtn4xc3.streamlit.app/)

## Project Overview
The Aviation Crisis Management Simulation is a Business Intelligence (BI) dashboard built to analyze and visualize the operational and financial impacts of flight cancellations, diversions, and weather delays across US airports. 

This tool empowers stakeholders to understand the "domino effect" of weather delays and identify critical bottleneck airports, ultimately estimating the financial revenue lost during aviation crises.

## Live Demo
**View the live dashboard here:** [Aviation Crisis BI Dashboard](https://aviationcrisismanagementsimulation-g8tdpjvenk879p6dtn4xc3.streamlit.app/)

## Key Features
* **Financial Analysis:** Visualizes estimated airline losses (due to cancellations) vs. airport gains (due to diversions).
* **Operations & Bottlenecks:** Tracks the domino effect of weather delays on taxi-out times and highlights the top 10 airports with severe taxi-in delays.
* **Geographic Crisis Explorer:** An interactive map powered by Geopy and Plotly, plotting crisis zones, cancellation volumes, and danger zone radar charts.

## Technology Stack
* **Language:** Python
* **Web Framework:** Streamlit
* **Data Manipulation:** Pandas, NumPy (Data heavily optimized via `.parquet` format for cloud deployment)
* **Data Visualization:** Plotly Express, Plotly Graph Objects
* **Geocoding:** Geopy (Nominatim API)
